## Lucid Agent â€” The Future AI Assistant

The **Lucid Agent** is LucidOS's built-in agentic AI assistant. In Alpha 0.1, it's a safe terminal placeholder that shows you what it will become.

To try it:
```bash
lucidos-agent
```

Or click **"Lucid Agent"** on the desktop.

### What makes it safe
- All commands are classified (allowed/confirm/blocked)
- Dangerous commands are blocked outright
- Risky commands require your explicit confirmation
- Everything is logged
